Utt-Anthariya

Utt-Anthariya is a clothing and footwear E-Commerce website for Khadi
products. Khadi represents our India and its simplicity and this motivated
us to do this project. We made the title from two Sanskrit words,
"Uttariya" which means a loose piece of upper body clothing worn during
ancient India and "Antariya" which means a lower body garment also worn
during ancient India.

Technologies used - HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL and XAMPP

This website could accommodate two different types of users, Admin and Customer.

  • A customer's interface included Women's Section, Men's Section, Unisex Section, Donate Section, Cart, Log-In & Sign Up.
  • An admin could login, manage (edit, delete & adding new products) individual sections separately and could view the list of customers.
